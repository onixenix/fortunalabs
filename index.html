How ApexTrend works

ApexTrend waits for the perfect alignment of trend, momentum, and breakout — then strikes. It's patient, selective, and exits fast when conditions change.

1

Entry — wait for the apex

All four conditions must align before entering a long position. No signal fires until trend, momentum, breakout, and volume all agree.

Price above rising 236 EMA
EMA 10 above EMA 38
Price breaks 35-bar high
Volume above 0.8× average
2

Hold — ride the trend

Average hold time is roughly 2 days. The wide ROI target (54.4%) and stoploss (-26.6%) let trades breathe through normal volatility. Most exits come from the signal, not the safety nets.

3

Exit — fast when momentum fades

When price closes below the 13 EMA on a 4-hour candle, the trade is closed. This is a fast, responsive exit that locks in gains before a pullback erases them.

Quick setup

1

Copy ApexTrend.py into your FreqTrade strategies folder.

user_data/strategies/ApexTrend.py
2

Copy config.json into your FreqTrade user_data folder (back up your existing config first).

user_data/config.json
3

Download 4h candle data and run a backtest to verify.

freqtrade download-data --config user_data/config.json --timeframe 4h --days 290 freqtrade backtesting --config user_data/config.json --strategy ApexTrend
4

Start dry-run trading (paper trading, no real money).

freqtrade trade --config user_data/config.json --strategy ApexTrend
