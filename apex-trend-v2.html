How ApexTrend v2 works

A systematic trend-following strategy that waits for confirmed momentum on the 4-hour chart before entering breakouts.

1

Entry — wait for the setup

Price must be above a rising 156 EMA (longs) or below a falling 156 EMA (shorts). Fast momentum (EMA 9 > EMA 21) confirms direction, then price breaks a 20-bar high/low with above-average volume.

Trend EMA: 156 Fast EMA: 9 / Slow EMA: 21 Breakout: 20 bars Volume: 2x average
2

Manage — ride the trend

ROI targets scale from 18.8% down to 0% over time. The 25 EMA exit catches reversals. Stoploss at -3.4% keeps individual losses small.

3

Exit — trend reverses or target hit

Closes when price crosses the 25 EMA against the position, or when ROI target is reached. Average hold time is about 2 days.

Quick setup

  1. Copy ApexTrend.py to your FreqTrade strategies folder
  2. Copy ApexTrend.json to the same folder
  3. Set your config to use "trading_mode": "futures" and "margin_mode": "isolated"
  4. Run: freqtrade trade --strategy ApexTrend --config user_data/config.json
